Pepperoni Pizza

Description

A delicious lasagna straight from Italia for the lasagna lovers!

Easy to make and even more to eat :D

Ingredients

  • ½ cup water
  • ½ (12 ounce) can CONTADINA Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Buy Lasagna
  2. Open Lasagna
  3. Heat up Lasagna
  4. Eat Lasagna
